About Trackitivity:

Trackitivity is a responsive WebApp that helps the user keep track of all the calories he burned during his workout.

Our user can search for the activities he executed during the workout and see how far he was from is goal!

The formula is a revision of the Harris Benedict Method, which can tell you what your basal metabolic rate is, and if you don't know what BMR is then we can help with that too -> Click Here
